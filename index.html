About Me

I am Sunday Temitope Toba, and just like the vast and diverse continent of Africa, my journey has been an intricate variety of challenges and resilience. From navigating economic disparities to bridging the ever-widening technological divides, I have been driven by a relentless passion for innovation and a steadfast commitment to making a difference. Much like AltSchool Africa, my story is one of empowerment, and innovation, and the AltSchool Africa Software Engineering Program represents an opportunity for me to up-skill.

The AltSchool Africa Software Engineering Program represents an avenue for me to delve deeper into the heart of technology. I plan to arrive at my destination as a highly-competent Software Engineer with modern skill-set.
